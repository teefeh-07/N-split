# Frequently Asked Questions

## General

### What is SereneHub?

SereneHub is a comprehensive DeFi platform built on the Stacks blockchain, offering NFT marketplace, token launchpad, staking, and service registry features.

### Which wallets are supported?

We support Leather (Hiro Wallet), Xverse, and WalletConnect-compatible wallets.

## NFT Marketplace

### How do I mint an NFT?

Connect your wallet, navigate to the Marketplace, click "Mint NFT", upload your artwork, and confirm the transaction.

### What are the fees?

The platform charges a 2.5% fee on all NFT sales.
